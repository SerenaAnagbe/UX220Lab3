Best Movie

Everything Everything

The life of a teenage girl who never left her home. This movie is about a teenage girl who never had the opportunity to leave her house. She lived a very restricted life with daily health routines to ensure she was safe and was not in contact with any bacteria.

After meeting and building a relationship with a neighbor that newly moved into the neighborhood, she was inspired by love to be braver and explore the outside world for the first time since she was a baby.

Over time, she realized that her mum had been lying to her about having a health condition that required her to stay indoors all the time because of her fear of losing another family member after the death of her husband.
